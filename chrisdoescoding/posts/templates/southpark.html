What is this?

Back in 2014, Hulu became the exclusive online streaming service for all seasons and episodes of South Park. Prior to that, all episodes were available for free on SouthParkStudios.com.

The killer feature back on SPS was the ability to click "Watch a Random Episode". For certain fans like me, I've already watched every single episode at least once, and watching them in sequence doesn't really appeal to me anymore.

As of this writing, Hulu does not and continues to not provide an episode randomizer feature. This page here provides a launching point to randomize an episode. Each click of the "Go!" button or the button below will take you directly to Hulu. You will still need access to a Hulu Plus subscription to watch the episode.
